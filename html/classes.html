Our Studio classes

Level up
Strong and fiery station training especially for an advanced level! In this training we will work in a cyclical way, when we will have several stations that will last a long time with intense exercises aimed at bringing the muscle to maximum exhaustion. The level up training is suitable for training at an advanced level who want to continue to challenge themselves and of course improve their physical fitness.

Triple work
Medium high intensity training, aerobic and strength together, combined work on stomach, buttocks and hips. The accessories we will use in the lesson are rubber bands, dumbbells and more. Suitable for all those looking to work hard, shape and firm these areas of the body!

Pilatis
The Pilates method is a theory of exercises, named after Joseph Pilates. In the class we will focus on working on the center of the body and the core muscles, while strengthening and lengthening the muscles. We will improve the posture of the body, holding the body and increasing the range of motion and flexibility in the muscles and joints. This class is suitable for anyone who wants to re-educate the muscles for proper posture and normal body movement. thus maintaining a strong, healthy and shaped body.

Extreme trampoline
An especially high-intensity burning class that combines strength and aerobic work together. Trampoline training helps burn increased calories and contributes to improving cardiopulmonary endurance. The combination of strength in a sculpting class shapes and helps weight loss.

band training
The training straps allow us to use our body weight as a resistance force. Using the straps we will work in a focused and strong way, on the upper body, lower body and abdomen and thus activate all the muscle groups in the body. The training is a combined aerobic and strength training together. Fenced as a medium difficulty workout and suitable for those who want to get stronger and shape up!

Suspension fit
Training with unique straps that allow work on body weight, body strengthening and shaping. In this training we will work with training straps that we can adapt to all levels from easy to hard. This class is considered the most popular among gyms in Israel and around the world due to its many benefits for strengthening the body. Highly recommended! Please note that sometimes other accessories will be included in the training according to the instructor's decision.
