About AY Gym

The AY Gym is a premier fitness complex that combines the best of humanity and technology. Our luxurious boutique-style club is designed to provide you with an exceptional fitness experience. Step into our high-quality studio rooms equipped with state-of-the-art fitness equipment, where you can work out and achieve your health goals.

Experience the ultimate convenience and comfort at AY Gym. We offer international standard showers and toilets, ensuring a refreshing and hygienic environment for our members. After your workout, head over to our healthy beverage bar to refuel and recharge. With a wide range of nutritious options, you can enjoy a post-workout drink that suits your taste and dietary preferences.

At AY Gym, we believe in catering to the diverse needs of our members. Choose from a variety of services that include apparatus pilates, invigorating cycling lessons in our dedicated studio, and studio lessons tailored to different fitness levels. Our unique color-coded method makes it easy for you to select the level of difficulty that suits you best.

Experience the difference of personalized attention at AY Gym. Our skilled and professional staff are here to assist you on your fitness journey. Whether you prefer one-on-one guidance or a group setting, our luxurious personal training room offers a comfortable and supportive environment for you to achieve your fitness goals.
